Create Your First Shortcut

Click the SmartText icon in your toolbar, then add a shortcut:

  1. Click + Add Shortcut
  2. Enter a key (e.g., addr)
  3. Type your expanded text
  4. Click Save

{"{"}{"{"}email{"}"}{"}"} Your email
